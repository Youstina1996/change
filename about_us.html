Save Our Planet

On our website, we want to shed light on one of the issues that occupy the world's thinking at the present time, which is the issue of climate change.
Our team works to encourage young people to submit their projects related to reducing climate change, increasing sustainability, and contributing to preparing a better future for future generations.
The website aims to hold a competition to present these projects every six months, and the first, second and third place winners are allowed to present their projects at the COP Annual Conference.
